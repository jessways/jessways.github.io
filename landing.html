Art Showcase

I have been exposed to various art projects since primary school
mostly on traditional fine arts.

Most used Media

The drawings shown below were done with mixed media such as acrylic, poster colour, water colour and ink or ink pen. Feel free to see more of my artworks on instagram https://www.instagram.com/hamless_sandwich/

Murals

I recreated Van Gogh's 'Potato Eaters' on my primary school wall (Balestier Hill primary School) when I was at the age of fourteen. This was my third mural painting that I was very proud of after many attempts of painting other Van Gogh's arts which were not as great. It is definitely challenging as my right hand was fighting against gravity when painting murals. The fourth painting that I did for a friend was a landscape of France with an Eiffel tower at the background.

Manga Style

'Manga' is a style of Japanese comic and graphic novels which is enjoyed by people of all ages even in Singapore. One of many reasons why I love to draw in manga style because it allows me to express my feelings, emotions and moods. I enjoy drawing human features the most especially without having to keep relying on real photos 1:1 and use more of my imagination.
